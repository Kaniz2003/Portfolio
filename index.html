Grandma's Remedy Bot

Grandma's Remedy Bot is a smart, multilingual chatbot designed to provide simple and effective home remedies — just like grandma used to recommend. It supports voice search and text-to-speech for a natural user experience and integrates WhatsApp and SMS reminders using Twilio to help users stick to their remedy schedules. The bot makes traditional healing more accessible and interactive, especially for the elderly or those who prefer natural solutions.


Driver Drowsiness Detection System

Real-time driver drowsiness detection is a car safety system designed to prevent accidents caused by driver fatigue. The project captures images every two seconds to detect facial changes using a webcam, identifying closed eyes as abnormal behavior. Upon detecting such actions, it generates an alarm to alert the driver. This project aims to enhance road safety by addressing the critical issue of driver drowsiness, which can lead to severe accidents.
